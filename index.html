Questions

The things people ask first.

Is my data safe with an AI system?
Yes. Every system we build is configured for zero data retention where it's available, meaning your inputs aren't retained or used to train any AI model. For law firms, this is documented in writing and built to your confidentiality obligations.
Do I need to be technical to use this?
No, and that's the entire point. We build the system, integrate it with the tools you already use, train your staff, and then run and maintain it for you. You use it. We handle the technology.
Is using AI in a law firm even ethical?
It can be, when it's set up correctly. Our founder holds a Juris Doctor, so your AI is configured and documented to your state's Rules of Professional Conduct and ABA Formal Opinion 512, including confidentiality, supervision, and a clear policy for when not to use AI.
What does it cost?
Engagements start with a one-time build fee and an optional monthly fee to run and improve the system. Founding-client pricing currently starts at $2,500 for a compliant setup. Exact pricing is scoped to your needs in a free assessment, with no hourly invoices and no surprises.
How long until it's working?
Most builds go from kickoff to go-live in about four weeks, depending on scope and how many tools we're integrating with.
Do I have to be at the Lake of the Ozarks?
Our focus is the Lake and the surrounding Missouri communities, where we work in person. Remote engagements are considered case by case.
