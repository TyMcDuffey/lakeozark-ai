The real problem

Guests expect a hotel's response time from a one-person operation.

A guest messages at 11pm asking about the dock, the Wi-Fi password, or where to put the trash. Another wants early check-in. A third left a lukewarm review you need to answer before it costs you bookings. Meanwhile a unit needs to be flipped before tomorrow's arrival and the owner wants last month's numbers. On a busy Lake weekend, that's not an inbox, it's a second job, and the platforms quietly reward hosts who reply fast with better placement.

You can keep doing it by hand and burning out, or hand it to a generic bot that sounds nothing like you and breaks on anything unusual. We do the third thing: a system built around your properties, your house rules, and your voice, configured carefully and run by a real person who's at the Lake. The routine stuff handles itself. The judgment calls still come to you.

Questions hosts ask

Straight answers before your next turnover.

Does it work across Airbnb, VRBO, and direct bookings?
Yes. We connect to the booking platforms and the property management system you already run, so guests get fast, consistent answers no matter where they booked. If you don't have a PMS yet, we'll recommend one that fits the size of your operation.
Will guests know they're talking to AI?
For routine questions, the system replies in your voice instantly, and most guests just experience a host who answers fast. You decide which messages send automatically and which get flagged for you to handle personally. Anything sensitive or unusual comes to you first.
Is my guest data safe?
Yes. We configure systems for zero data retention where it's available, so guest information isn't stored or used to train any model. You own your data and your guest relationships, full stop.
Will this work with Hostaway, Guesty, OwnerRez, or my current tools?
In most cases, yes. Part of the free assessment is mapping exactly what you use today so we build around it rather than asking you to start over.
I only have a couple of units. Is this worth it?
Often, yes, because with a small operation you are the messaging, the turnovers, and the reviews. Automating the routine layer gives you your evenings back. We'll tell you honestly in the assessment whether it pays off at your scale.
What does it cost?
A one-time build fee plus an optional monthly fee to run and improve it. Founding-client pricing currently starts at $2,500, with exact scope set in your free assessment. No hourly invoices.
